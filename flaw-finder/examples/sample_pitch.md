# Flaw Finder

Drop in a pitch deck, email, proposal, or website/app link. Agents roleplay as
a skeptical investor, a hostile lawyer, and a confused end-user. Each reviewer
punches holes in the idea, then a synthesis agent gives founders the highest
leverage fixes to make before they share it with the world.

Early users can upload a deck or paste copy and get a quick report in minutes.
The first customer segment is solo founders and small teams preparing for
investor, customer, or partner conversations.
